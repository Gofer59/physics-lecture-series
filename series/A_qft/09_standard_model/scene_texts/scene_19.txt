We started with path integrals. We met propagators and Feynman rules. We renormalized divergences. We summed loop corrections. We built gauge theories. Yang-Mills gave us the structure of the strong and weak forces. Spontaneous symmetry breaking and the Higgs gave us masses.

Today we assembled the full Standard Model. Its content, its successes, and its gaps.

You now hold the best-tested theory humans have ever written, in its correct form.

Nine lectures. A century of work. And we still do not know what most of the universe is made of.
