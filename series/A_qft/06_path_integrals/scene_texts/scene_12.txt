The path integral is invariant under shifts of its integration variable. Shift phi by an infinitesimal amount and out falls an exact equation relating correlators: the Schwinger-Dyson equation.

Roughly, the classical equation of motion holds inside correlation functions, up to contact terms that appear when the field coincides with another insertion.

This is the non-perturbative quantum generalization of the classical equations of motion. It holds to all orders, exactly.
