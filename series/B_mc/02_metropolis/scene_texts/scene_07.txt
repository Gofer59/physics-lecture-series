The simplest practical choice. Propose the current state plus a Gaussian bump of width sigma. Symmetric proposal, so the acceptance collapses to the minimum of one and the density ratio. Always accept downhill in energy.

Tuning sigma is everything. Too small and the chain crawls. Too large and every proposal is rejected.

The optimal acceptance rate for high-dimensional random walk Metropolis is twenty-three point four percent. Not twenty, not twenty-five. Derived from a Langevin limit. Someone's PhD.
