For the two-dimensional Ising model on the square lattice, the exact critical temperature is two J divided by k-B times the logarithm of one plus the square root of two. Roughly two point two six nine in coupling units.

Measure energy per spin, magnetisation, specific heat from the variance of energy, and susceptibility from the variance of magnetisation. Let them tell you where the phase transition lives. Onsager, nineteen forty-four.
