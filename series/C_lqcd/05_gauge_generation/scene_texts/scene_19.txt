Lattice gauge configurations are generated by Markov chain Monte Carlo. For pure gauge theory we use Metropolis, heat bath, and overrelaxation.

For dynamical fermions, the pseudofermion trick converts the determinant into a bosonic action. Hybrid Monte Carlo evolves that action by molecular dynamics plus a Metropolis accept-reject.

Modern tricks — multi-step integration, mass preconditioning, multigrid solvers — make physical pion mass simulations routine. Autocorrelation and topological freezing remain the open problems.
