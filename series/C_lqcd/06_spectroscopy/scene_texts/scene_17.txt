Beyond ground-state hadrons lies a rich spectrum. Radial excitations — pi, pi of thirteen hundred, pi of eighteen hundred. Exotic states — the X, Y, Z tetraquarks. Glueballs, predicted but elusive. Hybrid mesons with explicit gluonic content.

The variational method plus smearing machinery extracts their masses.

Lattice predictions for the glueball spectrum drive experimental searches. For candidates like the X of three eight seven two, the lattice tests molecular versus compact tetraquark interpretations.
