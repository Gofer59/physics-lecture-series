The Electron-Ion Collider, coming online at Brookhaven in the 2030s, will probe the internal structure of the nucleon and of nuclei at unprecedented resolution. Gluon PDFs. Generalized parton distributions. Transverse-momentum-dependent PDFs. Exotic hadrons.

Lattice QCD and the EIC are a natural partnership. The lattice predicts what the EIC should see. The EIC measures what the lattice must reproduce.

Much of today's hadron structure lattice physics is explicitly targeted at this machine.
